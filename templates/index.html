How the Machine Learning Works

A simple AI Fair explanation for the training pipeline.

01

Collect interactions

Imported playlist songs, manual songs, Likes, and Dislikes become training signals.

02

Build item features

Songs are represented by genre, artist, tags, energy, tempo, mood, and popularity buckets.

03

Train LightFM

LightFM learns user preference weights using a hybrid recommendation model.

04

Rank songs

The app combines model score and content similarity, then shows the Top-N results.
